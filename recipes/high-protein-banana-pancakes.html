High Protein Banana Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are so good, they'll surely satisfy any sweet tooth. Once you try them, you'll be shocked to know they're actually good for you!

ingredients

  • 1 T butter
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 super ripe banana (the riper the banana, the sweeter the pancakes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 T honey

Steps

  1. Allow skillet to heat on medium for 2 min
  2. While your skillet is coming to temp, mash your banana and mix in the eggs and cinnamon
  3. Once your skillet is ready, put just enough butter to prevent sticking and pour a small amount of batter into the pan (these are more flimsy than regular pancakes, I found it's easier to flip them if they are a little smaller)
  4. When you see them begin to bubble in the middle, they're ready to flip
  5. After about 1-2 minutes, they should be done, repeat steps 3-4 until batter is gone
  6. Drizzle with honey and enjoy!
