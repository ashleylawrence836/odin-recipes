Chicken and Rice

Description

This delicious chicken and brown rice recipe is quick and easy. All you need are a few ingredients, an instant pot, and about 30 minutes for a healthy, flavorful, single portion meal that'll leave you satisfied!

Ingredients

  • 1 T garlic powder
  • 1 T Onion powder
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  • chili flakes (however much you prefer, this is also optional)
  • 2 T extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 large chicken breast
  • i can of diced chili ready tomatoes in tomato juice
  • 1/2 cup dry brown rice

    • Note: I marinade my chicken overnight in teryaki sauce which contains a lot of salt. Due to the high salt content in the marinade and salt in the canned tomatoes, I do not add additional salt.

Steps

  1. Set your instant pot to saute and allow to come to heat for several minutes
  2. Add extra virgin olive oil, onions, and garlic, sautee until soft and fragrant, do not let the garlic burn!
  3. Filet your chicken and add to pot, turning after it browns
  4. Add your can of tomatoes followed by rice and seasonings
  5. Add the lid and set to pressure cook for 22 minutes
  6. Once the timer goes off, vent, if the rice is still slightly chewy, close the lid and add 5 more minues
  7. Check that it's seasoned to your liking and enjoy!
