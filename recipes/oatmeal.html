Oatmeal

Description

Want a simple, healthy breakfast? This quick bowl of oatmeal is one of my favorite go to breakfasts!

ingredients

  • 1 T butter
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 T brown sugar
  • 1 cup milk of choice (I use unsweetened almond) or water
  • 1/2 cup 100 % whole grain oats

Steps

  1. Heat your milk/water and butter in a pot until boiling
  2. Add oats and reduce to medium, cook 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Remove from heat, cover for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add brown sugar, stir, and top with fruit (optional) and enjoy!
